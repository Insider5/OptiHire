About OptiHire

Transforming recruitment with AI-powered intelligence

Our Mission

OptiHire is revolutionizing the recruitment industry by leveraging cutting-edge AI technologies to create perfect matches between talented candidates and innovative companies. We believe that finding the right job or the right candidate shouldn't be a game of chance—it should be powered by intelligent algorithms that understand skills, experience, and potential.

AI-Powered Matching

Our semantic matching engine uses Sentence-BERT to analyze resumes and job descriptions, creating accurate compatibility scores.

Smart Resume Parsing

Advanced NER (Named Entity Recognition) automatically extracts skills, experience, education, and certifications from resumes.

Interview Preparation

AI-generated interview questions tailored to each job and candidate help both parties prepare effectively.

Comprehensive ATS

Full applicant tracking system for recruiters to manage candidates through the entire hiring pipeline.
